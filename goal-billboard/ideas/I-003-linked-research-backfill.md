# I-003 — Backfill linked_research across goals

Today only ~1 of 13 active goals carries `linked_research` in its front-matter,
so research (205 docs) is the most under-connected type on the Board. The Board's
"Loose threads" saved view surfaces exactly which research *should* be linked to
a goal but isn't — turning a silent gap into a visible, fixable to-do. This idea:
walk that list and backfill `linked_research:` on the goals, strengthening the
existing org rather than replacing it. A data-hygiene pass, not a build. Captured
for review — not greenlit.
